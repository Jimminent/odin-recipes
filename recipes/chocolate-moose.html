Chocolate Moose

Home

Description

A delcious meal known well for its bold boldness! This is the confection of choice for many, including parents wanting a less religious Easter-Egg Hunt, hunters looking to spice up their wall-mount collection, and even rich Canadians.

I'll never forget my first chocolate moose. The smell of the rich chocolate mixed with the deep musk of moose-fur. The sound of its hooves clomping over the pine needles as it chased me through the forest. The taste of victory. The subsequent dysentery. Not a beginner recipe.

Ingredients

  • 40 gallons of melted Chocolate (preferrably milk)
  • 1 Live Moose
  • 4 cans of Moose Bait

Steps

  1. Lure the moose into your kitchen using the moose bait.
  2. Cover the moose with melted chocolate, using a baking spatula to spread the chocolate around.
  3. Enjoy. Serves 21
